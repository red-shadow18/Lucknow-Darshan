General Information:

Lucknow is the capital city of Uttar Pradesh. It is an important center of governance, administration, education, technology and culture. THe city stands at an elevation of 123meters above sea level. Approximate area of lucknow city is 631km2. Bounded on the east by Barabanki, on the west by Unnao, on the south by Raebareli and on the north by Sitapur and Hardoi, Lucknow sits on the northwestern shore of Gomti River.

Facts and Figures:

  • Lucknow's Bhool Bhulaiya is world's oldest manmade maze.

  • Lucknow's Naza market is India's 2nd largest IT market.
  • Lucknow's Husainabad clocktower is India's tallest clocktower.
  • Lucknow has humid subtropical climate.
  • Lucknow Metro is India's 8th longest operational metro.
  • Lucknow is also known as city of illusion.
  • Lucknow is one of the most literate cities of India.
